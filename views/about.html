About This App

Developer: Akira Sakaguchi (as1112@student.london.ac.uk)

About this app: <%= appName %> is a dynamic web application that functions as a dashboard to help you to control devices in their smart home.

It interacts with you to provide information related to the status of devices at home such as:

  • cooling and heating systems
  • curtains and blinds
  • lighting, security
  • audiovisual systems such as TV, radio, music players
  • kitchen appliances such as fridge-freezer, hob, oven, microwave, etc.
